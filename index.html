Magical catacomb
  • the goal for every player is to collect their treasures and then go back to their starting positions
  • the player who reaches their starting position first after collecting every treasure wins
  • the players can only collect their current treasures
  • every player has a turn, where they insert the current element (room) along an arrow, then move
  • the current element can also be rotated before it is inserted, the next current element will be the element on the other side, all other rooms move by one
  • the player can only move to rooms where a path leads, if the player does not want to move, they click on their current position
  • if a player is "pushed out", they appear on the other side, however, treasures are "pushed out" along with the rooms
  • there can be multiple players in one room
